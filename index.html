Who We Are

A Reliable Name in UAE Transport

Established in 2025, Farooq Khan Ayub Khan Transport & General Contracting is a customer-focused company delivering safe, efficient and cost-effective transport solutions across Abu Dhabi and the wider UAE.

From luxury chauffeur service and passenger buses to heavy equipment, cranes and general contracting support, we bring versatility, professionalism and dependability to every project — backed by a modern fleet and experienced drivers.
